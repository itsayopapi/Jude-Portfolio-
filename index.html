ABOUT

Jude Peter is a creative model with a cinematic eye—comfortable across lifestyle, street, and editorial looks. He blends calm presence with expressive storytelling, producing frames that feel alive, spontaneous, and memorable.

With over 8 years of experience in the fashion industry, Jude has worked with top brands and photographers worldwide, bringing versatility and professionalism to every project.

Driven by a passion for artistic collaboration, Jude continuously seeks to push boundaries and bring unique visions to life. His dedication to craft ensures every project is executed with precision and a distinct flair.
